What it takes to be a rapper

Being a rapper is no easy task, first the music industry is very oversaturated with many people competing to become the next upcoming rap super star, so the one thing you have to do is well being good at rapper, you have to learn the music scheme, rhyme schemes, how to catch and ride on a rap beat, how to have good music flow when you are rapping. Thats not the only thing you need to have good equipment for example a mic a music producing software like fl studio and more. Then you would need to put hours and hours into the studio so you can make good music with a good producer and enginerr to help make your songs better, then you will need to promote your songs and your brand and if your lucky enough you can sign to a label and get a good record deal or you could end up doing it on your own and be independent but a lot of people are not up to putting the work it takes to become a independent rap artist.
